Thanks for your interest in this study!


We are recruitimg pairs of participants for a short psychology study! We are particularly interested in recruiting pairs of participants that are emotionally close to one another: best friends, siblings, romantic participants, etc.

In this task you and your friend will take turns playing games on a computer. The study takes less than 30 min and each of you will recieve 5 dollars.

How to sign up: Please agree on a time with your friend before you sign up for the study! When you sign up, email amanda.royka@yale.edu with your friend's name and email and a set of times that you both are free in the next 7 days. You both will recieve a confirmation email with the time of your study slot. Note: That this study takes place in-person at the new psychology building at 100 College Street. Please make sure you budget enough time for you and your friend to get to the building in time for your study slot!
